Chapter - 15

Alignment Attribute

15.1. Types of alignment :-

15.1.1. Center :-

Aligns the whole text to the center of the web page

example :-

align = “center”

coding example :-

<html>
    <head>
        <title>Abhiram</title>
    </head>
    <body>
        <p align="center">Abhiram B S</p>
    </body>
</html>
output
