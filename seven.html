body tag

7.1. body tag :-

Everything inside this element is shown inside the main browser window.  <body> is opening body tag and
</body> is closing tag.

example:-

<html>
    <head>
        <title>Abhiram</title>
    </head>
    <body>
        <h1> Body of Page </h1>
    </body>
</html>
