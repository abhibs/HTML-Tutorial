21.1. <address> tag :-

The <address> ……. </address> tag is used to contain contact details for the author/owner of the page/article/web site.

coding example :-

<html>
    <head>
        <title>Abhiram</title>
    </head>
    <body>
        <address>
            Abhiram B S <br>
            S/O Srinivasamurthy B S <br>
            Javalli <br>            
            Tudoor P <br>
            Thirthahalli Tq <br>
            Shimoga D <br>
            577226
        </address>
    </body>
</html>
